Privacy Policy

How WTMG Labs collects, uses, stores, and protects personal data in GatemanAI, our physical-awareness service for entrances. Written plainly, because the people it affects should be able to read it.

Last updated: 27 June 2026

Who we are

GatemanAI is operated by WTMG Labs, based in Lagos, Nigeria. For the purposes of the Nigeria Data Protection Act 2023 (NDPA) and similar laws, WTMG Labs acts as the data controller for the information described below. You can reach us at aaademiluyi99@gmail.com.

What GatemanAI does

GatemanAI watches a single entrance (a gate, doorway, storefront, or perimeter point) for the owner of that location. When it detects motion, it captures an image, describes the scene using AI, and sends an alert to the owner over WhatsApp. The owner can also message GatemanAI to ask about recent activity, request a live view, or set standing rules.

What we collect

  • Entrance images. Still photographs captured at the monitored entrance when motion is detected, or on request from the account owner.
  • WhatsApp messages and phone numbers. The messages an account owner sends to GatemanAI, the replies we send back, and the phone numbers involved in delivery.
  • Event metadata. Timestamps, the site name, AI-generated scene descriptions, and related technical details for each event.
  • Account information. The owner's name, business name, email address, time zone, and the WhatsApp number that receives alerts.

How we use it

  • To detect activity at the entrance and send motion alerts.
  • To generate plain-language descriptions of what the camera sees.
  • To answer the owner's questions about recent activity and to provide a live view on request.
  • To run standing rules ("let me know if anyone arrives after 8 PM") that the owner sets.
  • To operate, secure, and improve the service.

We do not sell personal data. We do not use entrance images to build facial-recognition profiles or to identify individuals across locations.

Who we share it with

We use a small number of trusted service providers to deliver GatemanAI. They process data only on our instructions:

  • Google (Gemini API). Entrance images and text are sent to Google's Gemini models to generate scene descriptions and to power the assistant's replies.
  • Meta (WhatsApp Business Platform). Used to deliver alerts and to receive and respond to messages over WhatsApp.
  • DigitalOcean. Hosts the cloud service and database that store events and account data.

We may also disclose data where required by law or to protect the safety, rights, or property of users, the public, or WTMG Labs.

Where data is stored and for how long

Account data and events, including captured images, are stored in our cloud database. The on-site device keeps a short local audit log that is automatically deleted after 7 days. We retain pilot data for the duration of the pilot and delete or anonymise it when it is no longer needed for the purposes above, unless a longer period is required by law. An account owner can request deletion of their data at any time.

Security

Access to the cloud service is protected by authentication tokens, transport encryption (HTTPS), and access controls. Live-view requests between the cloud and the on-site device run over a private network. No system is perfectly secure, but we take reasonable measures to protect the data we hold.

Your rights

Under the NDPA 2023 and comparable laws, you may have the right to access the personal data we hold about you, to correct it, to request its deletion, to object to or restrict certain processing, and to lodge a complaint with the Nigeria Data Protection Commission. To exercise any of these rights, email aaademiluyi99@gmail.com and we will respond within a reasonable time.

A note on people captured at an entrance: GatemanAI is operated by the owner of the monitored location, who is responsible for using it lawfully and for any notices required where they operate. If you believe you have been captured by a GatemanAI deployment and wish to exercise your rights, contact us and we will route your request to the relevant operator.

Children

GatemanAI is a tool for business and property owners and is not directed at children. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children for our own purposes.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy as the service evolves. When we make material changes, we will update the date at the top of this page and, where appropriate, notify account owners.

Contact

Questions about this policy or how we handle data? Email aaademiluyi99@gmail.com.
